Identity

In crafting the visual identity for Am Design Studio, my personal initials "AM" from my name Ashley McAllen became the foundation. The journey of my logo’s design is a reflection of my professional evolution – it has been with me for many years, undergoing refinements rather than overhauls, showcasing my belief in the lasting power of a strong concept.

The emblematic feature of my brand's logo is the cloud that crowns the text. My fascination with clouds - their form, the science behind their formation, and their cultural symbolism - has deeply influenced this choice. They represent the transient and evolving nature of creativity, a perfect metaphor for the design process that transforms fleeting ideas into tangible art.

The stylistic alteration of the 'A', where its horizontal bar is replaced with a flowing, lowercase cursive 'm', symbolizes more than just a union of letters. It stands for harmony and continuity, principles I hold dear in my design practice. This fluid connection between the letters illustrates my approach to client relationships and project workflows – seamless, integrated, and cohesive.

I selected lavender for the 'm' to inject a sense of creativity and uniqueness into the logo, resonating with qualities integral to my work like elegance and introspection. Lavender is a color that communicates my individuality and the creative spirit I bring to every project. It also conveys a sense of calm and approachability, mirroring the experience I aim to provide my clients.

The ethos of my design philosophy is encapsulated in the simplicity and uniqueness of the logo. I believe that a minimalistic approach enhances recognizability and memorability – essential components of a strong personal brand. The simplicity is intentional, crafted to ensure the logo's adaptability across various mediums. Whether displayed on a business card, a website, or social media, it retains its visual impact and legibility, a testament to its thoughtful design.

Hello there!

I'm Ashley McAllen - an aspiring Graphic Designer & Web Developer with an interest in UX/UI design. I was born in California but moved to New York City when I was 8 and have stayed in New York since. I am currently a freelance Junior Graphic Artist studying at Southern New Hampshire University. I was lucky to find my passion for Graphic Design at a young age while attending the High School of Art and Design. I'm endlessly curious about the ability of effective, authentic communication and how visual storytelling can be used to enact change. As a design student, I am very committed and passionate to continue to learn, grow and enhance my design skills.
My skills include Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.
