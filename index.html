Hello there!

I'm Ashley McAllen - an aspiring Graphic Designer & Web Developer with an interest in UX/UI design. I was born in California but moved to New York City when I was 8 and have stayed in New York since. I am currently a freelance Junior Graphic Artist studying at Southern New Hampshire University. I was lucky to find my passion for Graphic Design at a young age while attending the High School of Art and Design. I'm endlessly curious about the ability of effective, authentic communication and how visual storytelling can be used to enact change. As a design student, I am very committed and passionate to continue to learn, grow and ehance my design skills.
My skills include Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.
