Paws and Claws Holistic Animal Clinic provides holistic veterinary care to our best friends.

They service a variety of different animals using natural care and services such as pet massages and acupuncture. They are located in the most pet-friendly neighborhood in Healthy Pets, USA. Paws and Claws Holistic Animal Clinic understands that your pets are valued family members and offers alternative and natural medical care that does no harm to your pets.

Looking Back

In this design, I skillfully applied the elements and principles of design to create a cohesive and impactful logo. I utilized clean lines and geometric shapes to create a balanced and easily recognizable image, and the strategic use of color not only adds visual appeal but also conveys the comforting and professional nature of the clinic. The symmetry in the design provides balance, while the proportion of elements like the sun rays and the animals within the logo reinforces the sense of harmony and unity. I ensured that the hierarchy was clearly defined, with the clinic's name prominently displayed, and I used repetition in the sun's rays to create a pattern that frames the logo, enhancing its visual rhythm. The contrast between the colors and the ample white space around and within the elements improves readability and allows for better scalability across various applications.
