M&M's Advertising Campaign

The M&M’s ad displays an advanced working knowledge of graphic design principles and software, effectively combining various elements to create a cohesive and appealing design that aligns with the brand's marketing strategy. The strategy was to create an ad for a new campaign based in diversity and inclusivity for the company. I used both Adobe Photoshop and illustrator to create this ad project. I believe my ad is a well-balanced composition. The placement of text, logos, and the central message was strategically organized to guide the viewer's eye through the content smoothly. I used to color to not only stay on brand with what M&M’s is known for but to also draw attention to the message while maintaining aesthetic appeal. For the different shaped candies, I used the pen tool. Photoshop was used to place the M&M’s logo and the individual m’s on the candies. I also used Microsoft PowerPoint to design the campaign presentation.
