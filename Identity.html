Identity

My logo is designed using my initials AM. I decided to remove the horizontal bar from the "A" and use a lowercase cursive "m" in its place. The color I chose for the "m" is lavender. I wanted my logo to be personal and memorable. By modifying my initials in this unique way, I aimed to create a visual representation of my brand. Removing the bar from the "A" and replacing it with a flowing lowercase cursive "m" signifies a sense of harmony and continuity between the letters, reflecting the seamless connection I want to convey. Lavender felt like the perfect choice for me. It's associated with qualities like creativity, uniqueness, elegance, and introspection, all of which resonate with my personal brand. Using lavender communicates my individuality and creative spirit while also suggesting a calm and approachable personality.

I believe in the power of simplicity and uniqueness in design. My logo's minimalistic approach and distinctive letter modification make it easily recognizable and memorable. Simplicity often leads to memorability, a crucial aspect of personal branding. I wanted my logo to be adaptable to various platforms and applications. Its simplicity and minimalism make it versatile, whether it's on my business card, website, or social media profiles. It maintains its visual impact and legibility across all these mediums.
