GRA 220 - Intro to Digital Imaging

Amethyst Bay Resort & Spa Assignment

Create a magazine print ad and a web banner ad. After choosing and ethically acquiring the digital materials that you want to use, prepare the imagery files in Adobe Illustrator (.ai) format and Photoshop (.psd) files and use the appropriate tools to implement masking techniques and adjustment layers to prepare images for use in the print ad design. You will apply the chosen client's typography and color palette and integrate all the chosen components together in a layout and structure appropriate for a full-page magazine ad design.

Reflection & Design Process

This project, one of my first since leaving High School, has taught me the profound impact that details can have in design, as well as the value of direct and constructive criticism. Such feedback has been pivotal in sharpening my design skills. My process involved the careful selection of royalty-free images and strict adherence to the project's specifications. The critiques and suggestions from my professor and peers were crucial, guiding me to resolve issues such as text readability against a busy background and overall composition within the ad. I faced challenges, like having to discard initial designs due to image quality and reworking the ads to align with the client's style guide. These hurdles highlighted the significance of image selection and meticulous attention to detail. Most importantly, the project reinforced our responsibility as designers to ethically source content and respect copyright laws, upholding our professional integrity. This experience has been incredibly instructive, blending creativity with technical rigor in design, and I am eager to apply these lessons in my future work.
